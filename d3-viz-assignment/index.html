June 1994 Flight Data

The below visualization contains data about the flights that happened in June 1994. Clicking on one of the edge nodes will change the visualization to only include that airport and any flights that go in and out of that airport. Due to a high number of flights and a relatively large dataset, colors are not shown until you dive deeply enough into the dataset(less 60 items).
